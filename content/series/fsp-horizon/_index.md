The platform I lead at Gomocha is twenty-five years old. It's running thousands of field workers a day. It works. And we're rebuilding it.

This series is the running commentary on that rebuild — the architectural calls, the multi-tenant pain points, the integrations that almost killed me, and the way an AI-native workflow changes what "tech lead" means when you're shipping a real product to real customers.

Episodes are written from inside the work, not after the fact. They're opinionated, sometimes wrong in retrospect, and always specific. No "10 lessons from rebuilding a platform" — just the lessons.
